# Manufacturing BOM Structure (MO Hierarchy Tree)

**Technical module name:** `bjet_mrp_bom_structure`

## What is this module for?

In complex manufacturing, a single product may contain:
- dozens of components
- subcomponents
- multi-level Bills of Materials (multi-level BOMs)

In standard Odoo, it can be difficult to quickly view the full structure of all materials required for production.

The **BOM Structure (MO Hierarchy Tree)** module solves this problem by allowing you to instantly view the complete hierarchy of all materials used in a product.

## What you get

- Full product material structure in one place
- Visualization of all levels of multi-level BOMs (1, 2, 3…)
- Quick component availability check
- More accurate procurement planning
- Automatic quantity recalculation when production volume changes

The module works in:
- **Manufacturing Orders** (MO)
- **Bills of Materials** (BOM)
